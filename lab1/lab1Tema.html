Why I like this genre

First and foremost, drum and bass is my favorite genre for the pure, unbridled energy it has. 170-175 bpm has an energy to it that no other genre has. Second reason is the incredible diversity the genre has. The only limitations are the standard breakbeat pattern (which still gets broken on a regular basis) and the bpm. If you want dubstep wobbles and fat rips, that works. If you want a light reese on top of a beat with a simple piano melody, that works. If you wanna have synths wailing over a strong bassline, that works. If you just want some chord stabs or saws over a breakbeat, that works. If you just wanna take a greasy bass and have it sit on one note and pitch bend down, that works. You can do truly anything with the genre.
